Our Services

Design Consultation

MackDecor provides design consultation services to clients who are seeking expert advice on their interior decoration projects. Our team of experienced designers will work closely with clients to understand their vision and provide tailored design solutions that suit their unique style, budget, and space requirements.

Space Planning

Space planning is an essential component of any interior design project. Our team of designers will work closely with you to understand your space and create a layout that maximizes its potential. We use the latest tools and techniques to create detailed floor plans and 3D renderings that help you visualize the finished project.

Color and Material Selection

Choosing the right colors is essential to creating a beautiful and cohesive interior design. Our team of designers can help you select the perfect color scheme for your space, taking into account factors such as lighting, mood, and overall style.

Furniture and Decor Selection

Selecting the right furniture is essential to creating a beautiful and functional interior. Our team of designers has access to a wide range of furniture suppliers and can help you select the perfect pieces to fit your style and budget.

Project Management

MackDecor offers project management services for interior decoration projects. Our team of experienced project managers will work closely with clients to ensure that every aspect of the project is executed smoothly and efficiently, from planning and budgeting to scheduling and delivery.

Commercial Design

Our commercial design services are designed to help businesses create beautiful and functional spaces that enhance the customer experience and drive sales. From retail stores to restaurants and hotels, our team of designers has experience creating stunning commercial interiors that are both practical and aesthetically pleasing.

Our Process

Consultation

Ideation

Design

Implementation

Client Satisfaction

We strive for 100% client satisfaction, and will work to make any necessary revisions.
